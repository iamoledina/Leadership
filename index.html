About the Leadership Lab

The 'Leadership Lab’ is a hands-on management training experience designed to empower small business owners and their teams with the practical skills to lead with confidence and inspire positive change in their organizations.

In today’s fast-paced economy, leadership isn’t just a skill—it’s a competitive edge that drives business success.

Investment

Full Access Package – $1,350

This package grants participants complete access to all 5 Leadership Lab sessions, including the in-person and virtual workshops. It is designed for business owners and managers who want to build essential leadership skills and implement them effectively in their organizations.

Leadership Mastermind Package – $1,800

This package includes everything from the Full Access Package, plus 3 additional mastermind sessions. These sessions are designed for participants to come together to discuss leadership challenges, share insights, and brainstorm solutions collectively. It's an opportunity to collaborate with peers, gain new perspectives, and receive group support on real-world leadership issues.

The Application Process

  • You are able and willing to actively participate, including having your camera on during virtual sessions, contributing ideas, and engaging in group work.

Steps:

  1. Complete the application form to express your interest and confirm your eligibility.
